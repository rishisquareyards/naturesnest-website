# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Families and professionals aged 35–55 in the GTA, household income $200K+, actively shopping for a move-up home. They are browsing on a laptop or tablet on a weekend morning, with intent — they've already shortlisted Oakville and are comparing pre-construction options. They care about school zones, community quality, long-term value, and builder reputation. They are not first-time buyers; they expect the site to match the quality of what they're buying.

## Product Purpose

naturesnest.homes is the dedicated marketing site for Nature's Nest at Ivy Rouge — 46 exclusive pre-construction freehold homes in Oakville, Ontario by Rosehaven Homes. The primary job is lead capture: get a qualified buyer to register their name, email, phone, and home type interest so the sales team can follow up with VIP pricing and floor plans. Secondary job: communicate that this is a premium, limited, trust-worthy opportunity — not a generic listing.

## Brand Personality

Grounded. Considered. Unhurried. The brand should feel like a luxury property brochure — not a real estate company website, not a tech startup landing page. The photography does the selling; the copy is precise and restrained.

## Anti-references

- Generic real estate sites (Realtor.ca, Zillow, any template-based developer site)
- SaaS-style layouts: metric cards, icon grids, feature tables
- Editorial-magazine aesthetic (Cinzel + ruled separators + italic drop caps + tracked all-caps on every section — this is what the AI default produces, avoid it)
- Cream / parchment / ivory / warm-neutral background (AI cliché of 2025–26)
- Any layout that looks like it was produced by an AI with no brief

## Design Principles

1. **Photography earns the headline.** The house renders and lifestyle images do the visual work. Typography is spare, purposeful, and gets out of the way.
2. **Limited means valuable.** 46 homes. The scarcity is the message. Don't bury it.
3. **Trust is built before the form.** By the time a user reaches the lead form, they should already feel this is the right builder in the right place at the right price.
4. **Restraint over elaboration.** One strong idea per fold. Resist the urge to fill every viewport.
5. **The form is a privilege, not a gate.** Frame VIP registration as exclusive access, not a friction point.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Full keyboard navigation. Reduced-motion support for all animations. Readable body text ≥16px with ≥4.5:1 contrast. Form labels always visible (not placeholder-only).
